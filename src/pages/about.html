About Us

Southern Philippines Institute of Science and Technology (SPiST) is a leading institution dedicated to excellence in science, technology, and innovation. Our goal is to provide top-tier education, equipping students with the expertise, skills, and values needed to succeed in today’s fast-evolving technological world.

At SPiST, we cultivate a dynamic learning environment that emphasizes research, hands-on training, and industry collaboration. Our diverse academic programs are designed to shape critical thinkers, problem solvers, and future leaders in science and technology.

Driven by our mission to develop skilled and socially responsible professionals, we are committed to offering state-of-the-art facilities, an innovative curriculum, and a faculty passionate about student success. Through our dedication to academic excellence and continuous growth, SPiST remains at the forefront of education, empowering individuals to create a lasting impact on society.

Join us in our pursuit of knowledge and innovation—where passion drives progress, and learning has no boundaries!
