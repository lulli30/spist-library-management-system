About Us

Southern Philippines Institute of Science & Technology (SPIST) is dedicated to providing quality education with a focus on science and technology.

Our library houses thousands of books, journals, and digital resources to support the academic needs of our students and faculty.

Our Team

Our library is staffed by professional librarians and assistants who are committed to helping students and faculty with their research and academic needs.
